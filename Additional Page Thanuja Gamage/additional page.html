Introduction to Classical Music

What is Classical Music?

Classical music is a genre of music that originated in Europe in the 18th century. It is characterized by its use of orchestral instruments such as the violin, cello, and piano, and its focus on melody, harmony, and structure.

Classical music is often associated with composers such as Mozart, Beethoven, and Bach, but there are many other composers and styles within the genre.

Famous Classical Music Albums

  • Beethoven's Ninth Symphony
  • Mozart's Requiem
  • Vivaldi's Four Seasons
  • Bach's Brandenburg Concertos
  • Tchaikovsky's Nutcracker Suite

Famous Classical Musicians

Itzhak Perlman

Itzhak Perlman is an Israeli-American violinist and conductor. He is widely considered to be one of the greatest violinists of all time, and has won numerous awards for his performances and recordings.

Lang Lang

Lang Lang is a Chinese pianist and music educator. He is known for his technical skills and passionate performances, and has performed with many of the world's leading orchestras.

Alison Balsom

Alison Balsom is an English trumpet player and music educator. She is known for her virtuosic performances and has won numerous awards for her recordings and contributions to classical music.

Benjamin Grosvenor

A British pianist, Benjamin Grosvenor made his debut with the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra at age 11 and has since gone on to perform with major orchestras around the world. He is known for his technical virtuosity and his ability to bring a fresh perspective to classical works.

Alina Ibragimova

A Russian-British violinist, Alina Ibragimova has won critical acclaim for her performances of both classical and contemporary music. She has recorded numerous albums and has collaborated with some of the world's leading musicians and orchestras.

Conrad Tao

An American pianist and composer, Conrad Tao made his debut as a pianist at age four and as a composer at age nine. He has since performed with major orchestras and has been praised for his innovative and genre-bending approach to classical music. He is also a prolific composer of contemporary classical music.
