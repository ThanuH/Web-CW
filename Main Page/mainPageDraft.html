Pop music is a popular music genre that originated in the mid-1950s in the United States and United Kingdom. It is characterized by catchy melodies, upbeat rhythms, and simple, relatable lyrics that are easy to sing along to. Pop music is known for its ability to appeal to a broad audience, often crossing cultural and language barriers. It has been influenced by a wide range of genres, including rock, soul, R&B, electronic, and hip-hop, and continues to evolve with new trends and styles. Pop music has produced countless memorable hits and has become a ubiquitous presence in modern popular culture.
